## My friends just sent me this picture of a coffee place they were at and told me to meet them at the pizza store across the street. What is the building number of that pizza shop?

### Hint: Flag should be in the format bronco{XXXX}, where XXXX is the building number.

Searched for this image and got the information from a website know as localwiki.

The coffee shop in the image is The Coffee Mill, located at 3363 Grand Ave, Oakland, CA.

Directly across the street is a Domino's Pizza located at 3360 Grand Avenue.

Therefore, the building number is: 3360

Flag : bronco{3360}
